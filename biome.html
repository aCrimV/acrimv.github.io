MINECRAFT BIOMES GUIDE

Overworld Biomes

Forest

Characteristics: Dense trees, flowers, wolves

Variants:
  • Flower Forest - Abundant flowers
  • Birch Forest - Birch trees only
  • Dark Forest - Dense canopy, mushrooms

Plains

Characteristics: Flat terrain, villages, horses

Features:
  • Best for building
  • Common village spawns
  • Sunflower Plains variant has sunflowers

Desert

Characteristics: Sand, cacti, temples

Resources:
  • Desert temples with hidden treasure
  • Husks (zombie variant)
  • Dead bushes for sticks

Special Biomes

Nether

Characteristics: Lava oceans, netherrack, fortresses

Variants:
  • Nether Wastes - Standard nether
  • Soul Sand Valley - Blue fog, skeletons
  • Crimson Forest - Red vegetation
  • Warped Forest - Blue vegetation

The End

Characteristics: Floating islands, endermen, chorus plants

Features:
  • End cities with valuable loot
  • Shulkers - levitating mobs
  • Chorus fruit - teleportation food
